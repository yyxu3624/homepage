About me:

I am currently a postdoc in the group of László Erdős at Institute of Science and Technology Austria (IST Austria).

Before that, I was a postdoc at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, working with Kevin Schnelli.
I obtained my Ph.D. degree in applied mathematics from University of California, Davis in 2018, under the supervision of Alexander Soshnikov.
I received my B.S. degree in applied mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013.

I am interested in probability theory, high-dimensional statistics, and mathematical physics, with a focus on random matrix theory.
