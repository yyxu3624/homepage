I am currently a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, working with Kevin Schnelli.
I am also a member of the group Random Matrices, Stochastic Models and Analysis (RMSMA).

Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in 2018 at the Department of Mathematics University of California, Davis under the supervision of Alexander Soshnikov.
I recieved my B.S. degree in Mathematics in 2013 from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

I am interested in Probability Theory, Mathematical Statistics and Mathematical Physics, with a focus on Random Matrix Theory.
