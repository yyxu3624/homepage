About me:

I am currently a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, working with Kevin Schnelli.
I am also a member of the group Random Matrices, Stochastic Models and Analysis (RMSMA).

Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in applied mathematics from University of California, Davis in 2018, under the supervision of Alexander Soshnikov.
I recieved my B.S. degree in applied mathematics from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2013.

I am interested in Probability, Mathematical Statistics and Mathematical Physics, with a focus on Random Matrix Theory.
